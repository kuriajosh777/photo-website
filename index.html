Biography

Josh Kuria

I'm Josh, a self-taught photographer based in Nairobi. I picked up a camera to slow down and pay closer attention to ordinary moments — a hallway of light, a stranger's expression, the last few minutes of the day. Most of the shots here were taken on long walks, with no plan beyond staying curious.

When I'm not shooting, I work in IT and administrative support, which might explain why I care about getting the small details right: focus, framing, and the moment just before someone stops posing and starts being themselves.
